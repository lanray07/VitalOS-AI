# StoreKit Product Placeholders

- `vitalos.premium.monthly`: Premium Monthly, £12.99
- `vitalos.premium.yearly`: Premium Yearly, £99.99
- `vitalos.elite.monthly`: Elite Monthly, £24.99

The app uses `StoreKitSubscriptionService` with StoreKit 2 product loading, purchase, restore, and entitlement refresh.

Before submitting a new binary:

- Confirm the Paid Apps Agreement is active in App Store Connect.
- Complete metadata for all three subscription products.
- Upload an App Review screenshot for each product.
- Submit the subscriptions with the app version for review.
