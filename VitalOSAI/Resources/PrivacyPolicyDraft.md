# VitalOS AI Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: June 10, 2026

VitalOS AI provides educational wellness tracking, check-ins, protocols, voice coach reflections, analytics, and subscription features. VitalOS AI is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, emergency support, or clinical monitoring.

## Data Collected

VitalOS AI may collect or store the following data in the app:

- Profile preferences: age range, activity level, wellness goals, and coaching style.
- Daily check-ins: mood, stress, focus, energy, sleep quality, motivation, and timestamps.
- Wellness records you choose to enter: sleep records, recovery scores, habits, protocols, projections, and analytics.
- Voice coach content: microphone audio is used to create a transcript when you choose to record; generated transcripts and AI responses may be saved in the app.
- HealthKit data: only if you grant HealthKit permission, VitalOS AI can read wellness data to personalize educational insights and can write wellness samples you choose to save.
- Purchase status: subscription entitlement state from StoreKit.
- Notifications: notification permission and reminders if you enable them.

## How Data Is Collected

Data is collected when you enter it in the app, grant Apple system permissions, record a voice coach session, enable notifications, or complete a StoreKit purchase.

## How Data Is Used

VitalOS AI uses data to:

- Generate educational wellness insights and adaptive protocols.
- Display analytics, habit trends, projections, and wellness summaries.
- Save voice coach transcripts and generated responses you create.
- Manage subscription access through StoreKit.
- Schedule reminders if notifications are enabled.

## Third-Party AI Services

VitalOS AI version 1.0 does not send check-ins, HealthKit data, voice transcripts, profile details, or other personal data to a third-party AI service. Wellness responses are generated in the app from local educational wellness rules.

If remote AI processing is added in a future version, VitalOS AI will identify the provider, disclose the data categories first, ask for permission before sending personal data, and require the provider to provide the same or equal protection for user data.

## Apple Services

VitalOS AI uses Apple frameworks including HealthKit, Speech Recognition, microphone permissions, notifications, and StoreKit. These Apple permission prompts are shown by the system before protected data or device capabilities are used.

## Data Sharing

VitalOS AI version 1.0 does not sell user data and does not share personal wellness data with third-party AI providers.

## User Control

Users can decline optional permissions, stop using voice coach recording, restore or manage subscriptions through Apple, and delete app data from Settings > Delete all data.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support contact listed in App Store Connect.
